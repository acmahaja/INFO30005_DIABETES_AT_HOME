Diabetes@Home

Diabetes management like never before! Manage your diabetes from the convenience of you own home and always keep in touch with your clinician.

Designed with simplicity in mind, Diabetes@Home empowers users by giving them a new way of recording their daily measurements, tracking their data over time, and receiving medical feedback.

With Diabetes@Home, recording your daily data is only ever one touch away.

Project Description
Perfect for clinicians

A special web interface enables clinicians to carry out their duties more effectively than ever before. Easily check your patients’ daily entries and comments, tailor records and thresholds for single patients, and never forget important details with the note-taking feature.

The messages feature enables patients to receive daily encouragements, helping them meet their goals and feel cared for.

Clinicians Login
Stay connected

Diabetes@Home includes a social aspect which is designed to improve user engagement and help patients feel connected. Special badges are awarded to users for meeting pre-set goals, and a leaderboard provides a feeling of pride and accomplishment to patients with the highest engagement rates. Never feel like you’re alone in this battle!
